Originated as Federal Roofing in 1944, Marshall Roofing & Sheet Metal has helped commercial and industrial building owners cope with New England weather for over 80 years.

We partner with clients to develop roofing systems best suited to their requirements and their budget. We also work with private owners and their representatives, general contractors, and specifiers, as well as federal, state and municipal contracting authorities.

Our portfolio of experience encompasses all of the major commercial and industrial roofing systems, and we are ANSI/SPRI ES-1 Certified to fabricate our own ES-1 compliant edge metal, through our wholly-owned subsidiary, Maplewood Sheet Metal.
